UwUify

UwUify takes a user inputted string and adds UwU and OwO in the place of certain parts of words, directly
after an e put wo, add uwu to the end of the sentence and in place of an o replace it with owo .

Input: UWUIFY "Hello my name is noah and I enjoy Coding!"
Output: "Hewo my namewo is nowo and I enjoy cowoding uwu!"
